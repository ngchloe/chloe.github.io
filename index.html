Occupant formula (Equation 4-1)

OLF = (0.00007)(GLA) + 2.3225

where:

OLF = The occupant load factor (square metres per person) (but not less than 2.787 sqm per person (30 square feet per person) and not exceeding 4.645 sqm per person (50 square feet per person).

GLA = The gross leaseable area (square metres)
